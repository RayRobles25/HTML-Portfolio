The Best Movies According to Ray

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Crazy Stupid Love

  • Crazy Stupid Love Trailer

You can never go wrong with this Star-Studded Cast. This Romantic-Comedy is guaranteed to turn that frown upside down.

Cinderella Man

  • Cinderella Man Trailer

This is the inspiring story based on a true-story about the life of James J. Braddock. If you are in need of some major motivation, especially if you're feeling in the dumps, this is the movie to watch.

The Notebook

  • The Notebook Trailer

Talk all the mess you want about this one, but true men aren't afraid to express their true love and do anything for the woman they love. This book is a testimony about the power of love. A must see.
